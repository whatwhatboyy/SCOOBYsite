Is Scooby safe to use?

Scooby is built with safety in mind and updated regularly to maintain undetected status. We continuously improve our protections to keep the experience smooth while minimizing risk.

What's the difference between Free and Premium?

The free version gives you a solid set of features across several games at no cost. Premium unlocks the full menu — heist editor, money recovery, modded vehicles and 1000+ features. Premium Plus adds BattlEye Enabled support for public lobbies and playing with friends.

Which games are supported?

GTA 5, RDR2, FiveM, RedM, CS2, GMOD and a standalone Spoofer + Cleaner are available now. PUBG, Rainbow Six Siege and S&box are in development.

Do I pay once or is it a subscription?

Lifetime licenses are a one-time payment — no recurring fees. Exact pricing depends on the game; open any product page for its current price. Crypto payments save you 20%.

Can I play with friends in public lobbies?

Yes. Scooby works in public lobbies with non-modders and friends — no isolation, no restrictions. GTA 5 Legacy and Enhanced are both supported, with BattlEye Disabled (Host) on Premium and full BattlEye Enabled support on Premium Plus.

How do I get support?

Join our Discord for instant help from the team and community, browse the Guides and Docs, or reach us on Telegram. Delivery is instant after purchase.
