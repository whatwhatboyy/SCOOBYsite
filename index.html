Yes, Scooby is designed with safety in mind. We regularly update our protection systems to ensure the best possible experience while minimizing risks.

The Free version includes most features like Player Options, Vehicle Spawner, Teleports, Weapons, Heist Editor, and Modded Outfits. Premium adds Business Tools, Lua Scripts support, and priority support for only $10 lifetime.

Yes! Scooby fully supports both GTA V Legacy and GTA V Enhanced editions. You can use the same menu on either version.
