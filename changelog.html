CHANGELOG

Version history and updates for Scooby GTA V Enhanced Edition

v2.1

Latest

COMBAT FEATURES

  • Aimbot - Multiple hit bones, adjustable FOV, activation modes, smooth aiming, auto-fire, visibility checks, target indicators
  • Silent Aim - Separate system with FOV targeting, shoot through walls option, custom FOV circle
  • Magic Bullet - Bullets hit through walls, adjustable damage, silent mode, target indicators
  • Triggerbot - Auto-fire on crosshair, burst fire mode, magnet mode with snap aiming
  • Hitmarkers - Visual/sound feedback, custom colors for hits/headshots/kills, rainbow mode
  • Damage Indicators - Floating damage numbers, critical hit colors, stack rapid damage

POWERS

  • Telekinesis - Grab entities, 10 action modes (Hold, Throw, Ragdoll, Spin, Orbit, etc.)
  • Forcefield - Push peds/vehicles away, adjustable radius and force
  • Aura - Visual aura effect, multiple types, rainbow mode, push entities
  • Electric Touch - Zap nearby entities with multiple effects
  • Flaming Fists - Explosive/burn/ragdoll on punch, one punch kill
  • Dash - Shift + WASD dash with invincibility and visual effects
  • Paraglider - Glide with boost/dive controls, auto-activate on fall
  • Shockwave Ball - Energy balls with Push, Pull, Bounce, Homing, Black Hole modes
  • God Slap - Dash and slap with explosive/burn/launch options
  • Walk On Air - Invisible platform at adjustable height
  • Matrix - Slow-motion bullet time effect

ESP FEATURES

  • Player ESP - Names, distance, skeleton, box, tracelines with custom colors
  • Ped ESP - Names, weapons, distance, skeleton, box, tracelines
  • Vehicle ESP - Names, distance, box, health display
  • Crosshair - Multiple styles (Cross, Circle, Dot, Spinning), rainbow fade
  • Health Bars - Position options, armor bar, custom colors
  • Box Settings - Full/Corners/3D, rounded corners, glow effects
  • Skeleton Settings - Outline, glow, head dot styles
  • Tracelines - Multiple origins, curve/fade/dashed options
  • Projectile Trails - Bullet/throwable trails, rainbow mode, impact boxes

WEAPON FEATURES

  • No Recoil / No Spread - Perfect accuracy
  • Custom Weapons - Gravity Gun, Vehicle Gun, Paint Gun with rainbow
  • Weapon Globals - Infinite ammo/clip, rapid fire, fire ammo, explosive ammo, damage multipliers

MOVEMENT & TELEPORT

  • Noclip / Freecam - Fly through walls with adjustable speed
  • Super Jump / Beast Jump - Enhanced jump abilities
  • Super Run / Superman - Enhanced speed and flying
  • Blink Teleport - Quick forward teleport with marker styles
  • Mark & Recall - Save and return to positions
  • Quick Teleports - Waypoint, objective, aim, vehicle teleports
  • IPL Loader - Load/unload interiors with teleport

UI & BUG FIXES

  • Organized ESP into tabs (Main, Crosshair, Debug, Visuals, Settings)
  • Organized Weapons into tabs (Main, Aimbot)
  • Fixed Triggerbot not detecting peds inside vehicles
  • Fixed Silent Aim functionality
  • Fixed Magic Bullet activation modes
  • Fixed ESP box settings display issues
  • Fixed multiple ImGui duplicate ID errors
