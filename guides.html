First Time Setup

When installing Scooby for the first time, follow these steps:

Step 1: Download

Head over to scoobymenu.xyz and find the download button. Download the menu using the Mega file. Make sure your anti-virus is turned off.

Step 2: Extract & Run

Extract the files into any folder. After that, run the .exe as an administrator. This ensures the launcher will run successfully.

Step 3: Install Requirements

Required Software:

VC Runtimes:

https://www.techpowerup.com/download/visual-c-redistributable-runtime-package-all-in-one/

DirectX:

https://www.microsoft.com/en-gb/download/details.aspx?id=6812

Make sure you have your graphics drivers up to date.

Step 4: Inject

Load up GTA and wait for it to be on the main menu or in story mode. Then go back to the launcher, get a free license, and follow the steps.

When you have acquired your free license, click Inject. This should inject the menu into the game. Then press Insert to open the menu - congratulations, you're done!

Modding Outfits

Modding outfits with Scooby is very simple! The menu includes a saved outfits section.

Using Shared Outfits

To use other people's saved outfits, find the file they share (many outfits can be found in the Scooby Discord) and save it to:

C:\scooby\GTA5\outfits

When you load in-game, use the menu to navigate to the outfits section and you will find the modded outfits there!

Creating Your Own Outfits

You can also mod outfits yourself! Head to the Outfit Editor in the Scooby menu. You can change the numbers on your character's clothing, and when you've found your perfect outfit, save it!

Modding Vehicles

Modding vehicles with Scooby is also very simple!

Creating Modded Vehicles

Open the menu and head to the Vehicles icon, then click Vehicle Editor at the bottom. This will allow you to mod your vehicle. To save it, head to Saved Vehicles, click Save Vehicle, name it, and save!

Using Shared Vehicles

Similar to outfits, you can use others' saved vehicles. Head to the Discord, find the vehicles thread, and download a file you want. Then drop it into:

C:\scooby\GTA5\vehicles

Money Making Methods

Within the Scooby menu there are many ways to make money if you don't want to do it legitimately.

Heist Method

Make sure to enable Solo Hosting in the Recovery tab. This lets you do heists that require more than 1 person. Head to the Heists tab, set up your heist accordingly, and start it. When the heist starts, click Insta Finish to end it immediately - you'll receive 100% of the cut!

Casino Method

Head to the casino and enable Manipulate Slot Machines in the Recovery > Casino option. Go to the slots, bet the max, and you'll hit the jackpot! Disable the option, force yourself to fail a slot, then turn it back on and repeat.

Business Method

Head to the Business tab on the menu and find the Fill Supplies button. Your supplies will be maxed out, ready for you to sell!

Using Scooby in Public Lobbies and with Friends!

Scooby V3 has introduced a new update, this involves the ability to play in public lobbies and play with friends.

How to Set Up

To do this you must join a public lobby and wait for everyone to get kicked out so you are the host of the lobby (YOU MUST BE THE HOST). After, you must turn on the Player Magnet found in the Network tab. This will make players join your lobby.

Playing with Friends

If you want to play with your friend, do the same steps and ensure you are the host. Then simply just invite them to the lobby.

What Works in Public Lobbies

The following features are fully functional in public lobbies:

  • Recovery Features - Money methods, heist editor, casino manipulation
  • Vehicle Features - Spawn vehicles, vehicle editor, saved vehicles
  • Outfit Features - Outfit editor, saved outfits, modded clothing
  • Player Features - Godmode, teleportation, super jump, etc.
  • World Features - Weather control, time control, clear wanted level
  • Weapon Features - All weapons, infinite ammo, no reload
  • Business Features - Fill supplies, instant production
  • Heist Features - Solo hosting, insta-finish, custom cuts

Important Notes

  • Always ensure you are the host of the lobby for best results
  • Use the Player Magnet feature to populate your lobby
  • Some features may require you to be in a solo public lobby first
  • Be cautious when using features around other players

Troubleshooting

Here are solutions to common issues you may encounter:

Failed to allocate memory in target process

Head to your Rockstar Launcher, click on Settings, and disable BattlEye. This will fix the issue.

Please return to Grand Theft Auto V (Black Screen)

You must inject the menu during the loading screen. This message means the game is signifying you're going back to story mode.

BattlEye is required to play GTA V Online

Same solution - inject the menu during the loading screen, not when you're already in the game.

Black screen with an error message

Restart your game without opening Scooby, go to story mode, and turn off Frame Generator in the GTA graphics settings.

Failed to write process memory

Turn off BattlEye in the Rockstar Launcher settings.

Launcher closes after opening

Use a VPN - your ISP may be blocking the menu. If that doesn't work, delete the C:\scooby folder to clear your cache, then restart Scooby.

Free key website unreachable

Your internet is blocking it - use a VPN.

Injection success but doesn't work in game

Use a VPN - your ISP may be interfering with the connection.

Injection Failed

There are several fixes:

  • Use a VPN
  • Ensure all anti-virus software is disabled
  • Reset the Scooby cache by deleting the C:\scooby folder and restarting
