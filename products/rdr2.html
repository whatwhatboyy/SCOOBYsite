Product Description

Overview

Scooby RDR2 is a premium Red Dead Redemption 2 Online mod menu packed with features across 6 main tabs. From self protection and weapons to world control, recovery, and a full Lua scripting engine, Scooby delivers the most complete RDR2 Online experience available.

Self & Player Options

  • Protection - God mode, no ragdoll, anti lasso, anti hogtie, anti melee, and anti AFK
  • Visibility - Invisible, off the radar, and never wanted
  • Health & Stamina - Keep all bars and cores filled, auto heal, infinite stamina, unlimited oxygen, fireproof, explosion proof
  • Movement - Super run, super jump, walk on water, swim fast, superman flight, noclip, and free cam
  • Tools - Clone self, NPC ignore, whistle override, player scale (0.1 - 5.0), slow motion, and eagle eye
  • Animations - Play any animation or emote with full dictionary support

Weapons & Combat

  • Ammo Types - Infinite ammo/clip, fire, explosive, electric, poison, penetration, and homing ammo
  • Accuracy & Damage - No spread, no recoil, steady aim, one hit kill, and damage scale overrides
  • Special Guns - Delete gun, teleport gun, gravity gun, freeze gun, heal gun, burn gun, launch gun, push gun, ragdoll gun, shrink/enlarge gun, and airstrike gun
  • Aimbot Suite - Full aimbot with FOV, smoothing, and auto fire. Silent aim with through-walls support. Magic bullet with custom range and damage. Triggerbot with burst fire and magnet aim
  • Combat Features - Auto cock, rapid fire, quick draw, keep guns clean, and infinite dead eye

Horse & Vehicle

  • Horse Protection - Horse god mode, no ragdoll, no fall damage, never buck
  • Horse Movement - Super run, infinite stamina, climb steep slopes, walk on water
  • Horse Appearance - Keep clean, flaming hooves, horse scale, and auto-filled bars and cores
  • Vehicle Options - Vehicle god mode, invisible, no collision, lock doors, super drive, fly mode, and speed boost

Teleportation

  • Quick Teleport - TP to waypoint, mount, Madam Nazar, camp, moonshine shack, and train track
  • Towns - Instant teleport to Valentine, Saint Denis, Blackwater, Rhodes, Strawberry, Annesburg, Van Horn, Tumbleweed, Armadillo, and more
  • Saved Locations - Save and organize custom teleport locations with categories

  • Player Actions - Spectate, ghost mode, teleport to/behind player, bring player, spawn gold chest, spawn bounty/hunting wagons
  • Trolling - Kill, explode, lightning, remote bolas, cages, attachments (spank, ride on shoulders, slap), kick off horse, and more

  • Moderation - Mark as modder, trusted flag, block join, and full player database
