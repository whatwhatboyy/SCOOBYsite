Product Description

Overview

Scooby GTA V is a premium modification menu designed for Grand Theft Auto V Online. Originally started in 2022 as a private project, it has evolved into one of the most feature-rich and user-friendly mod menus available. With over 1350+ features across 8 tabs and 70+ categories, Scooby provides everything you need for the ultimate GTA experience.

Self & Player Options

  • Godmode & Health - Godmode, no ragdoll, anti-headshot, health regen, auto heal, auto armor, and more
  • Movement - Super run, super jump, beast jump, noclip, and movement multipliers
  • Powers - Invisibility, off-radar, ghost mode, never wanted, and ped control options
  • Model Changer - Change your player model to any ped, animal, or custom model in the game
  • Outfit Editor - Access exclusive clothing combinations, rare items, and modded outfits
  • Teleportation - Instantly travel anywhere on the map with preset locations and custom coordinates

Weapons & Combat

  • Weapon Mods - Infinite ammo, rapid fire, explosive ammo, fire ammo, no recoil, no spread, and damage overrides
  • Aimbot Suite - Full aimbot with rage/legit presets, silent aim, triggerbot, and magic bullet
  • Custom Weapons - Gravity gun, vehicle gun, paint gun, teleport gun, delete gun, airstrike gun, money gun, and repair gun
  • Visuals - Laser sight, tracer rounds with rainbow mode, and explosive headshots
  • ESP System - Full ESP for players, peds, vehicles, weapons, and objects with box, skeleton, traceline, health bars, and chams
  • Crosshair - Custom crosshair overlay with full customization

Vehicle Features

  • Vehicle Mods - Vehicle godmode, speed boost, fly mode, and handling editor
  • Vehicle Spawner - Spawn any vehicle in the game with instant delivery
  • Vehicle Editor (LSC Style) - Full Los Santos Customs style editor for paint, wheels, performance, and more
  • Saved Vehicles - Save and load your favorite vehicle configurations
  • Handling Editor - Fine-tune acceleration, braking, traction, suspension, and all vehicle physics

Recovery & Money

  • Money Recovery - 500K and 750K money loops, nightclub safe loop ($300K/cycle)
  • Rank Editor - Set your rank instantly (1-8000), crew rank, LS Car Meet rep, and Arena War AP
  • Stats Editor - K/D editor, max all stats, and individual stat controls
  • Unlocks - Unlock all weapons, seasonal items, GTA+, and more
  • Cooldowns - Reset and bypass various in-game cooldowns

Heist Tools

  • Cayo Perico - Custom cuts, primary target selection, skip hacking, instant finish, and value editing
  • Diamond Casino - Player cuts, approach/target/difficulty selection
  • Doomsday - Full heist control with act selection and cut editing
  • Apartment Heists - Custom setups, loots, and player cuts
  • Mission Manager - Skip to checkpoints, instant finish, solo missions, RP/money multipliers

Business Manager

  • Business Controls - Register as CEO/VIP/MC President, claim safe earnings from all businesses
  • Nightclub, Bunker, MC - Manage stock, supplies, and production for all your businesses
  • Business Teleports - Instantly teleport to any owned business property

Network & Session

  • Session Control - Join any session type, join by username or Rockstar ID, fast join
  • Player Actions - Friendly options (heal all, give weapons, clear wanted) and lobby controls
  • Spoofing - Spoof your name, Rockstar ID, and other player info
  • Services - Request MOC, Avenger, Terrorbyte, Kosatka, and more instantly

Protection

  • Anti-Kick - Block all kicks including host kick, vote kick, script kick, and desync kick
  • Anti-Crash - Block all crash attempts, invalid models, invalid syncs, and task crashes
  • Anti-Modder - Detect modders, mark them on player list, auto-kick, and notification alerts
  • Event Filtering - Full script event and network event filtering with logging
  • Misc Protection - Block remote teleport, cages, bounty setting, sound spam, explosions, and more

World & Fun

  • World Control - Time/weather control, blackout, gravity modification, and chaos mode
  • Destruction - Black hole, tsunami, nuke, meteor, airstrike, angry planes, and fireworks
  • Spawner & Creator - Spawn and manipulate objects, peds, and props in the world
  • Lua Scripts - Extend functionality with custom Lua scripts and community-created content
