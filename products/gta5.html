Product Description

Overview

Scooby GTA V is a premium modification menu designed for Grand Theft Auto V Online. Originally started in 2022 as a private project, it has evolved into one of the most feature-rich and user-friendly mod menus available. With over 200 features, Scooby provides everything you need for the ultimate GTA experience.

Key Features

  • Heist Editor - Customize and modify heist payouts including Cayo Perico and Casino heists
  • Money Recovery - Safely boost your in-game finances with our recovery options
  • Modded Vehicles - Spawn and customize vehicles with exclusive modifications
  • Modded Outfits - Access exclusive clothing combinations and rare items
  • Teleportation - Instantly travel anywhere on the map
  • Player Options - Godmode, super jump, fast run, and more
  • Vehicle Options - Vehicle godmode, speed boost, fly mode
  • Lua Scripts - Extend functionality with custom scripts

Compatibility

Scooby works with both GTA V Legacy and GTA V Enhanced editions. It's designed to work in public lobbies, allowing you to play with friends and other players. The menu supports BattlEye Disabled (Host) and BattlEye Enabled modes.
